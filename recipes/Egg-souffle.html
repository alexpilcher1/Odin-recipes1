Egg Souffle Recipe

About the Egg Souffle

The egg souffle is a very simple dish, with many possible ingredients depending on what sort of flavor you like. I personally like a basic combination of ingredients in order to make this quick, and still provide a lot of flavor!

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup of mixed vegetables
  • 1/2 lb of ground beef

Steps

  1. Begin by panfrying the ground beef until browned, then drain liquid and add seasoning as desired.
  2. Crack the raw eggs into a metal pan, and add the cooked ground beef.
  3. Combine the cheese and mixed vegetables into the pan as well, and stir until it is mostly consistent (some of it may sink, that is okay).
  4. Add the cup of milk and stir even more, until there is a nice spread of the meat and vegetables.
  5. Place pan into oven at 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  6. Take the pan out of the oven, the top should be brown when finished
  7. Allow to cool for at least 5 minutes, then slice into however many pieces you would like.
  8. Enjoy this delicious and easy to make meal! The leftovers taste just as good microwaved the next day.
